Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your information

Effective Date: 1st January 2025  ·  Last Updated: June 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to InstantProject ("we", "our" or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our academic writing and research services.

By accessing or using InstantProject, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms stated here, please discontinue use of our platform.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide to us when you place an order, including:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Academic details (department, level of study, research topic)
  • Order-specific instructions and uploaded guideline documents
  • Payment transaction references (processed securely by Paystack — we do not store card details)

We may also automatically collect certain technical data when you visit our site, including your IP address, browser type, device type and pages visited. This data is used solely for analytics and platform improvement purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To process and fulfil your academic service orders
  • To communicate with you regarding your order status and delivery
  • To send you receipts and transaction confirmations
  • To respond to your enquiries and provide customer support
  • To improve our platform, services and user experience
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations

We do not use your information for advertising profiling or sell it to third-party marketers under any circumstances.

4. Payment Information

All payments on InstantProject are processed through Paystack, a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway. We do not collect, store or process your debit or credit card details directly. Payment data is handled entirely by Paystack in accordance with their own privacy and security standards. Please review Paystack's Privacy Policy at paystack.com for further details.

5. Confidentiality of Orders

We treat every order placed on InstantProject with strict confidentiality. Your research topic, academic details, uploaded documents and any instructions you provide are used solely for the purpose of fulfilling your order. This information is not shared with other students, third parties or any external organisations.

Our team members who handle orders are bound by confidentiality obligations and are prohibited from disclosing any order-related information.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Trusted third-party providers (such as Paystack for payments and email delivery services) who assist us in operating our platform, bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order or governmental authority to disclose information, we will do so only to the extent legally required.
  • Protection of rights: If we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property or safety of InstantProject, our users or the public.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, or as required by applicable law. Order details may be retained for a period of up to 12 months for quality assurance and dispute resolution purposes, after which they are securely deleted.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal data, subject to our legal obligations
  • Withdraw consent for non-essential data processing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@instantproject.ng.

9. Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies to enhance your browsing experience and maintain session functionality. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes. You may adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies, though this may affect certain features of the platform.

10. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration or destruction. All data transmissions on our platform use SSL/TLS encryption. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites and encourage you to review their privacy policies independently.

12. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of InstantProject after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@instantproject.ng
