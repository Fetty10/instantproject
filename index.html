Got questions?

We have answers.

Everything you need to know about ordering, payment and getting your document.

💬
How do I get my document? ▼
Fill the form, complete payment and your document downloads instantly — right to your device. No waiting, no back and forth.
What is the cost? ▼
Pricing is shown automatically on the form once you select your service and chapters. The total is calculated based on exactly what you order before you pay.
Can I buy just one chapter? ▼
Yes! Select the chapters you need individually. Once you pick individual chapters, the "Complete Project" option is disabled — and the other way round too.
How many pages will I get? ▼
For a complete project, minimum pages depend on your level of study — ranging from 60 pages for diploma levels up to 200 pages for PhD. Individual chapters follow standard academic research page counts.
What format is the document in? ▼
Every document is formatted in Times New Roman, font size 12, double line spacing and justified alignment — the standard format accepted by most academic institutions.
Which referencing styles do you support? ▼
APA (7th Edition), Harvard, MLA, Chicago, Vancouver, OSCOLA (Law), IEEE (Engineering) and Turabian. You can also type in your own institution's specific style.
How do I pay? ▼
Payment is handled securely online. You can use any debit card, bank transfer or USSD. Your card details are never stored on our platform.
Is my information kept private? ▼
100%. Your name, topic and order details are never shared with anyone. Everything is confidential — we take your privacy seriously.
What if I need something specific? ▼
Use the Guidelines field to describe any special requirement — supervisor instructions, institution format, page count or deadline. We factor everything in.
Can I get a refund? ▼
If your document was not generated due to a technical issue, contact us within 24 hours and we will fix it or refund you. Once a document is downloaded, it is non-refundable. See our full Refund Policy.
