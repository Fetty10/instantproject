Refund Policy

Fair, transparent and straightforward

1. Our Commitment

At InstantProject, we are committed to delivering quality academic materials and a seamless user experience. We understand that issues can occasionally arise, and we have designed this Refund Policy to be fair to both our users and our business.

Please read this policy carefully before placing an order. By completing a payment on our platform, you acknowledge that you have read and agreed to the terms of this Refund Policy.

2. Instant-Download Services

For services where the document is automatically generated and made available for download immediately upon payment (including Projects, Thesis chapters, Dissertations, Term Papers, Seminar Papers, Proposals and Journal Articles), the following applies:

✅ Eligible for refund: Technical failure where payment was deducted but no document was generated or made available for download.
❌ Not eligible for refund: Once a document has been successfully generated and downloaded, no refund will be issued. This is because the digital product has been delivered and accessed.

If you experienced a technical issue during download, please contact us within 24 hours of your payment with your transaction reference, and we will either re-generate and deliver your document or issue a full refund at our discretion.

4. Non-Refundable Circumstances

Refunds will not be issued in the following circumstances:

  • The document was successfully generated and downloaded, regardless of the user's satisfaction with the content
  • The user provided incorrect information (wrong topic, department, level, email address) at the time of ordering
  • The user changed their mind after the document was generated
  • The user's institution rejected the document — academic outcomes are outside our control
  • The document did not pass a plagiarism or AI detection check — as clearly stated in our Terms of Service, we do not guarantee detection outcomes for auto-generated documents
  • The refund request is made more than 72 hours after the original payment
  • Duplicate payments made in error — in this case, only the duplicate transaction will be refunded; the original order payment is non-refundable once the document has been accessed

5. Duplicate Payments

If you accidentally made a duplicate payment for the same order, please contact us immediately at refunds@instantproject.ng with your transaction references. We will verify the duplicate and process a refund for the extra payment within 5–7 business days, depending on your bank or card issuer.

6. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  • Send an email to refunds@instantproject.ng within the applicable timeframe
  • Include your full name, email address used at checkout, Paystack transaction reference and a clear description of the issue
  • Attach any relevant screenshots or evidence of the technical failure

We will acknowledge your request within 24 hours and provide a resolution within 3–5 business days. Approved refunds are processed back to your original payment method and may take an additional 3–7 business days to reflect, depending on your bank.

7. Chargebacks

We strongly encourage users to contact us directly before initiating a chargeback with their bank. Initiating a chargeback without first attempting to resolve the issue with InstantProject may result in the suspension of your account and may be disputed by us with supporting evidence of service delivery.

8. Changes to This Policy

InstantProject reserves the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of our platform after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
